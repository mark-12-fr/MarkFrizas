03. about

About Me

I'm a Full-Stack Developer and Founder of MJR Vertex, building production applications that solve real problems for educational institutions. My flagship product, AcadTrack — an AI-powered Academic Management System — is deployed across schools in Visayas and was showcased at the 2nd INNOVEX 2026 International Conference. The system handles enrollment, grading, and real-time learning analytics for thousands of students.

I specialize in Python + Flask, PostgreSQL + Supabase, and vanilla JavaScript. Whether it's real-time messaging (Socket.IO), biometric systems (fingerprint enrollment), or complex educational workflows, I build across the entire stack — from database architecture to pixel-perfect UI.
